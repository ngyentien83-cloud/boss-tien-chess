How to Play

The Basics

Play chess against Boss Tien, a cursed AI opponent. Click a piece to select it, then click a highlighted square to move.

Ego Points

Earn ego points by making moves (+1) and capturing pieces (+8). Spend them in the Ego Store to activate powerful abilities.

Boss Tien's Powers

👁 Metavision — Tien predicts your threats. Glowing blue squares reveal his awareness.
🔴 Domain Expansion — When Tien's energy is high, the board transforms and hazard zones appear.
💀 Ghost Squares — Where Tien captures, ghostly remnants block movement temporarily.

Your Abilities (Ego Store)

🟢 Simple Domain — Clears all hazards and ghost squares.
🌀 Force Flow State — Triggers endgame intensity early.
🔥 Hinokami Kagura — King emits a burning aura that destroys adjacent enemies.
⚡ Thunder Breathing — Move twice in one turn (no capture on second move).
